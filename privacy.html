Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30 December 2025


1. Data Processing

Utility Deck is a client-side tool. This means that for most operations, your data does not leave your browser.


  • Passwords: Generated locally on your device. We do not see, store, or transmit them.

  • Files: Hash calculations happen within your browser. Files are not uploaded to any server.



2. External Services

To provide Network Intelligence, we query public APIs (such as ipify, icanhazip, ipapi.co) to retrieve your connection details. These requests are made directly from your browser to the service provider.



3. No Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking pixels. We do not collect personal analytics.
